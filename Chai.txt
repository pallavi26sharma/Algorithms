Recipe (algorithm) to make a delicious cup of tea.
Step 1: Take a pan.
Step 2: Take one fourth cup of water.
Step 3: Pour it into pan.
Step 4: Put that pan on gas and turn gas on.
Step 5: Now add 1 tablespoon sugar in the water.
Step 6: Add half tablespoon of tea leaves.
Step 7: Let the mixture boil for about 2 minutes.
Step 8: Now take one cup of milk and add it to the mixture.
Step 9: Let the mixture boil for 5 to 6 minutes.
Step10: Now turn off the gas and our tea is ready.
Step11: Take a strainer and pour the tea through it into cup.
Step12: Our tea is ready to be served.
